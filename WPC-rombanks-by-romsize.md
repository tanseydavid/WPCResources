WPC ROM Banks by ROM size
=========================

### 128KB ROM size
* 8 total ROM banks
* Range: `$38` - `$3F`

### 256KB ROM size
* 16 total ROM banks
* Range: `$30` - `$3F` 

### 512KB ROM size
* 32 total ROM banks
* Range: `$20` - `$3F`

### 1MB ROM size
* 64 total ROM banks
* Range: `$00` - `$3F`
